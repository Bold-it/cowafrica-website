Join Our Mission

At the Center for One Welfare Africa (COWAfrica), we are uniting passionate individuals to advance One Health principles across the continent. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a dedicated student, you can make a difference through our Country Ambassadors & Representatives or Student Interns & Ambassadors programs. Join us to advocate for human health, animal welfare, and environmental conservation, and help create a healthier world.

Our Programs

Country Ambassadors & Representatives

Lead the charge for One Health in your country. Our Country Ambassador Program seeks passionate leaders to represent COWAfrica at a national level, advocate for interconnected health solutions, foster partnerships, and drive impactful initiatives. Ideal for professionals with a commitment to health and sustainability.

Student Interns & Ambassadors

Gain hands-on experience and make a difference as a student. Our Student Internship and Ambassador Program offers university students and recent graduates opportunities to work on One Health projects, develop professional skills, and expand their networks. Perfect for young leaders passionate about health and conservation.
