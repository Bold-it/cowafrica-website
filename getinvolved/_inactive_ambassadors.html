Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a paid role?

The Country Ambassador role is a volunteer position. However, ambassadors may receive support for specific initiatives or events based on funding availability.

What are the main responsibilities of a Country Ambassador?

Country Ambassadors advocate for One Health principles, build local partnerships, lead community initiatives, and represent COWAfrica at national events and forums.

Do I need prior experience in One Health to apply?

While prior experience is beneficial, it is not mandatory. We value passion, leadership skills, and a commitment to advancing health and sustainability in your country.

What is the application deadline?

We review applications on a rolling basis. Positions are filled as needed, so we encourage you to apply as soon as possible.

How will I be notified about my application status?

All applicants will receive email notifications regarding their application status. If you haven't heard from us within four weeks of your application, please feel free to contact us at info@cowafrica.org.
