Who We Are

Center for One Welfare-Africa (COW-Afri) is a non-profit organization dedicated to advocating, promoting, and safeguarding the ‘welfare of all’ through resilient implementation of One Health System through multisectoral collaborations. We recognize that the earth is one, and life revolves and thrives in one ecosystem and biodiversity; Human Health, Animal Health, and Environmental Health are inextricably linked and hinged.

Mission Statement

COW-Afri aims to reduce animal suffering, enhance human health outcomes, and safeguard the environment by collaborating with diverse expertise, promoting effective strategies, and implementing holistic solutions through strong leadership and management.

Vision Statement

We envision a sustainable future in which the health and well-being of humans, animals, and the environment are interconnected and mutually supportive, fostering thriving ecosystems and flourishing communities across Africa and beyond while augmenting the strenuous efforts aimed at the timely achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Our organization operates as a hub for knowledge exchange, capacity building, and advocacy, working to bridge gaps between disciplines and sectors to address complex health challenges holistically.

Core Values & Principles

Policy & Advocacy

Engage with opinion leaders and decision-makers to advocate for our ideas and proposals for implementation.

Rigorous Applied Research

Conduct comprehensive and high-quality investigations to gain new insights and perspectives that will help us achieve our objectives.

Extensive Education & Communication

Engage in comprehensive teaching and learning across diverse subjects and mediums to foster lifelong growth.

Stakeholder Engagements & Collaborations

Collaborate effectively with various authorities and experts to foster innovation, learning, development, and a greater sense of achievement.

Excellence & Adaptability

Committed to achieving the highest quality in all pursuits, while being proactive and resourceful in new environments and capable of recovering from setbacks.

Respect & Equality

Treat all personnel, clients, and stakeholders with respect, valuing their backgrounds and skills, and create an environment with equal opportunities for all to achieve our goals.

Volunteerism & Optimism

Freely provide extensive expertise, resources, and services to support good causes, navigate challenges with resilience, and promote proactive problem-solving in communities.

Innovations & Technology

Approach problems with a new perspective, explore innovative ideas, and discover unique solutions that lead to new opportunities and advancements.

Compassion

Treat everyone with kindness, valuing their emotions to foster a safe and valued atmosphere, while making meaningful impacts.

Ethical

Ensure all actions align with moral principles and established standards, making decisions based on recognized values.

Data Sharing

Exchange data resources with external sources, applications, users, or organizations to collaborate, build partnerships, and create new opportunities while maintaining data integrity.
