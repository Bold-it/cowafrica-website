Country Ambassadors & Representatives

Our Country Ambassadors and Representatives play a vital role in advancing COWAfrica’s mission across Africa. They advocate for the One Health approach, foster local partnerships, and engage communities to promote human, animal, and environmental welfare.

Our Ambassadors

Ngadie Koroma

Country Ambassador - Sierra Leone

A Veterinarian and an Animal Science specialist, with varied years of theoretical and hands-on experience, who is passionate about teaching and promoting the application of scientific principles to enhance animal productivity, health, and welfare. I believe in a collaborative approach to animal health, as it is linked to human health, ensuring that pet owners and livestock farmers are well-informed and actively involved in their animals' health decisions. I am steadfast in staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in animal health through continuous learning and professional development. My passion lies in fostering the well-being of animals and building strong relationships with their owners.

ROSALINE VIRGINIA BUNDEH

Country Representative - Sierra Leone

A protean Public Health practitioner ardent about applying in-depth knowledge and skills in preventing and combating diseases and disasters;- empowering the community in healthy behaviors, fighting for young people, being the voice for women and girls, combating poverty, and improving the quality of life of members of society. As an active writer with high proficiency in Epidemiological Methods and Biostatistics, I want to use research to advance the health systems, as I am drawn to strategic healthcare planning to deliver quality care for all - these have been my incentives to be an active member of society.
