Stay updated on COWAfrica’s upcoming events, including workshops, webinars, and conferences focused on advancing the One Health approach. Join us to collaborate with experts, communities, and stakeholders in addressing interconnected health challenges across Africa.

2025 Upcoming Events

One Health Summit 2025

August 10-12, 2025 | Nairobi, Kenya

Join us for the One Health Summit 2025, where over 400 participants from across Africa and beyond will gather to discuss innovative strategies for integrating human, animal, and environmental health.

Key features:

  • Keynote addresses from global One Health leaders
  • Workshops on zoonotic disease prevention
  • Networking sessions for cross-sector collaboration
  • Launch of the 2025 One Health Action Plan

Webinar: Climate Change and Zoonotic Diseases

June 15, 2025 | Online

This webinar will explore the impact of climate change on the spread of zoonotic diseases, featuring experts in epidemiology, veterinary science, and environmental health.

Highlights:

  • Panel discussion with leading researchers
  • Case studies from Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Interactive Q&A session
  • Free resources on climate-health strategies

Community Health & Welfare Workshop

July 20-22, 2025 | Kampala, Uganda

This workshop will train community leaders and health workers in One Health principles, focusing on improving health outcomes in rural communities.

Objectives:

  • Train 50 community health workers
  • Develop local health action plans
  • Promote integrated disease surveillance
  • Provide tools for sustainable health practices

Wildlife Conservation & Community Engagement Project

September 1-15, 2025 | Maasai Mara, Kenya

This field project will engage local communities in wildlife conservation efforts, focusing on protecting habitats and reducing human-wildlife conflict.

Planned activities:

  • Establishment of 5 community wildlife corridors
  • Training for 100 local conservationists
  • Workshops on sustainable farming practices
  • Monitoring of wildlife populations
