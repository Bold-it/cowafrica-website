Community Health Outreach in Akwamufie & Kyeredorm, Ghana

November 21, 2020

In collaboration with Loveworld Church, COWAfrica conducted a health outreach in Akwamufie and Kyeredorm, Eastern Region, Ghana, screening 268 participants for HIV, Hepatitis B, and Malaria. The program, led by Richard Klottey Botchway and Fredricka Nayram Affrram, provided health education, counseling, and referrals to VRA Hospital. Volunteers from VRA Hospital, including Bertha Akorfa Agba, played a key role.
