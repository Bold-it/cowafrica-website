What We Do

Human Health & Well-Being Advocacy

We work to improve access to healthcare, promote wellness, and address social determinants of health in communities across Africa, ensuring a holistic approach to human well-being. Our initiatives focus on delivering healthcare services, educating communities on disease prevention, and advocating for equitable health policies.

Core Areas

  • Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)
  • Health Systems Strengthening
  • Maternal & Child Health
  • Mental Health
  • Food Safety & Security
  • Social Equality & Network
  • Inclusion of People Living with Disabilities (PWDs)

Animal Health & Welfare Advocacy

Focus on improving animal health, preventing zoonotic diseases, and advocating for humane treatment of animals, fostering a balanced ecosystem. Our initiatives include vaccination drives, training programs for farmers, and research to understand and mitigate disease transmission between animals and humans.

Core Areas

  • Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR)
  • Animal Health Systems Strengthening
  • Zoonotic & Neglected Tropical Diseases (NTDs)
  • Animal Welfare Standards
  • Wildlife Management & Conservation

Environmental Health & Conservation Advocacy

Promote sustainable practices, protect natural habitats, and combat climate change to ensure a healthy environment for future generations. Our initiatives focus on reforestation, sustainable agriculture education and practices, and advocacy for policies that reduce environmental degradation.

Core Areas

  • Wildlife Management & Conservation
  • Climate Change
  • Environmental Pollution
