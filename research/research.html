Advancing One Health Through Research

At COWAfrica, our research drives impactful solutions at the intersection of human health, animal welfare, and environmental conservation across Africa.

Our Research Mission

COWAfrica is committed to advancing the One Health framework through rigorous research. We focus on understanding the interconnected challenges of human, animal, and environmental health to develop evidence-based solutions that benefit communities across Africa.
