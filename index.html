Our Mission

The Center for One Welfare—Africa is dedicated to promoting, optimizing, and balancing the health and welfare of humans, animals, and the environment; ensuring a harmonious ecosystem through collaborations and integrations across diverse disciplines, research, evidence-based policy advocacy, capacity building, strong leadership, and effective management, thus holistically preventing and mitigating complex health risks arising from the human-animal-environment interface across Africa, and beyond.
